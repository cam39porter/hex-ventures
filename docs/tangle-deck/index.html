User Acquisition

The power of pull social

Start with non-scalable initiatives for user acquisition

  • Campus events at Princeton, NYU, and Columbia for promotion and feedback

  • Delight our users by sending printed Tangle visualizations to early adopters

  • Collison installation / contain the fire to make it incredibly hot – Paul Graham

Promote acquisition and retention loops in each feature

  • Captures: @mention via Tangle usernames, email, or phone

  • Surface: Publish Tangle visualizations, digests, and captures to social media

  • Tangle: Invite new users to group or individual Tangles and get a visualization print

  • Reflect: Send valuable daily digests and gentle notifications to motivate users to capture thoughts

  • Inspire: Keep users excited by surfacing valuable thought paths and old ideas that relate to their current thinking
